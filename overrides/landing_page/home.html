  • Statistical Rigor

    Your analyses are built on our peer-reviewed publications. The harsh reality of SAE in data sparse situations is that there will often be large uncertainty in area-specific prevalences, and we take great care to accutrately characterize that uncertainty.

  • Accessible Hands-On Analysis

    The sae4health R Shiny app transforms complex statistical analysis into an accessible, user-friendly experience. No prior programming or deep statistical knowledge needed. Detailed documentations, step-by-step tutorials and an intuitive interface guide users through the entire analytical process.

  • Creative Data Visualization

    Our surveyPrev R package and the sae4health app offer powerful visualization tools that turn data into actionable insights. Explore and interpret subnational variations through interactive maps and charts.

  • Collaborative and Evolving Ecosystem

    Our platform enables a dynamic and collaborative ecosystem, with educational sessions, partnerships across global agencies and country teams. It has been featured in WHO-led workshops and other technical settings, evolving through user feedback and continuous updates.
