    Statistically Rigorous Small Area Estimation

    Powered by SurveyPrev and the R Shiny App.

    Innovated by the STAB Research Group
    at University of Washington.

    Get started Learn more

    • Statistical Rigor

      Our R package and Shiny app leverage advanced statistical methods for subnational estimates of key demographic and health indicators from DHS and MICS data. Your analyses are built on our peer-reviewed publications.

    • Accessible Hands-On Analysis

      The Shiny app transforms complex statistical analysis into an accessible, user-friendly experience. No prior programming or deep statistical knowledge needed. Detailed documentations, step-by-step tutorials and an intuitive interface guide users through the entire analytical process.

    • Creative Data Visualization

      Our R package and Shiny app offer powerful visualization tools that turn data into actionable insights. Explore and interpret spatial distributions through interactive maps and charts, enabling clear inferences for public health decisions.

    • Collaborative and Evolving Ecosystem

      Our software is part of a dynamic ecosystem, offering educational sessions, collaborations with country offices, and continuous updates. We evolve the app based on user feedback, empowering research, informing policy, and driving impactful public health outcomes.
